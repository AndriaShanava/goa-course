Lunkarya Guinea Pig

The Lunkarya is a unique breed of guinea pig, known for its long and curly fur, which gives it a distinct appearance compared to other guinea pig breeds. Originating from Sweden, the Lunkarya is a result of a natural mutation, and today it is loved for its luxurious coat and friendly temperament. They require regular grooming to maintain their beautiful curls and are known for their outgoing and social personalities, making them great pets.
