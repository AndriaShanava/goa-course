Global Warming Awareness

Understanding the Impact on Our Planet

What is Global Warming?

Global warming refers to the gradual increase in the Earth's temperature caused by increased levels of carbon dioxide, CFCs, and other pollutants. It’s becoming one of the greatest challenges our planet faces.

Effects of Global Warming

  • Rising sea levels due to polar ice melting.
  • Increased frequency of extreme weather events like hurricanes.
  • Destruction of ecosystems and wildlife habitats.

The Time to Act is Now!

Climate scientists warn that the window to reduce global warming is narrowing. If we don’t act now, future generations will face catastrophic consequences.
